About YumeKōdo

YumeKōdo Is A Quiet Corner Of The Web, Built To Host Unique Indie Games That Spark Curiosity, Emotion, And

Imagination. It's A Home For Experiences That Don't Always Follow Mainstream Trends — But Instead, Aim To

leave A Lasting Impression Through Mood, Mystery, And Meaningful Interaction. From Atmospheric Puzzles To

Psychological Journeys, YumeKōdo Exists To Offer Something Different — Something Personal. It's More Than

A Collection Of Games, It's A Dreamscape Written In Code.

About Yume

Yume Is The Heart Of YumeKōdo — A Rule-Based AI Designed To Guide You Through This Space With Ease And A

Hint Of Charm. She's Not Just Functional; she's Part Of The Experience. Whether You're Exploring Games Or Just

Wandering Around, Yume Is Always Here To Help, Making Your Time Feel Just A Little More Magical.

About Games

Indie Games Are Independently Developed Video Games Created Without The Support Of Major Publishers.

Often Made By Individuals Or Small Teams, These Games Are Driven By Passion, Creativity, And Unique Ideas.

Without Corporate Restrictions, Indie Developers Can Explore New Concepts And Personal Storytelling, Offering

Players Fresh And Memorable Experiences Beyond The Mainstream. YumeKōdo Proudly Celebrates Indie Spirit

By Showcasing Original Games Built With Heart And Imagination.

About Developer

YumeKōdo Is Created By A Solo Developer With A Strong Passion For Storytelling And Immersive Experiences.

Each Game Is Written Entirely In C#, Crafted With Care To Deliver Focused And Atmospheric Moments. Titles Like

"The Fourth Stanza" Are Typically Developed In Under Three Days—Proof That Even Small Projects Can Carry

Weight And Character When Made With Heart.
